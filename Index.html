Prompt TGE & Potential Earnings Estimator

This tool analyzes your current Prompt points (obtained via API) and combines it with the following considerations to provide simplified potential earnings estimates. Remember, these are estimations based on assumptions and not financial guarantees.

Key Considerations & Assumptions:

  • Total Prompt Tokens: 1 Billion
  • Total Prompt Tokens Allocated for Cachers: 400 Million
  • Assumed TGE (Token Generation Event) Unlock Percentage: 25%
  • Total Tokens Disbursed *at* TGE: 100 Million (25% of 400 Million)
  • Assumed post-TGE monthly Prompt points contribute to potential future monthly token drops (details TBD by the project).
  • Assumed tokens (both at TGE and potentially monthly) are distributed linearly based on points (e.g., Your Points / Total Points * Tokens Available). No tiering or slabs are considered in this simple model.
